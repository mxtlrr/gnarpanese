# Gnarpanese Affixes

## Suffixes
| Suffix | Meaning of the (new) word |
| ------ | ------------------------- |
| -orp   | The word is an adjective. |
| -z     | [Noun] Plural             |


### The noun suffixes
There are multiple suffixes, that, when added to a word,
will classify said word as a noun. They are:
- `-eble`
- `-eby`
